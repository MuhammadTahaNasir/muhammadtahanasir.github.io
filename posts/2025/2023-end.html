Exam Craze and Semester Farewell

سلام, ٹیک فیم! Finals week is upon us, and I am prepping like crazy, fueled by coffee and sheer panic. Books and notes are scattered everywhere, marking the end of Semester 1. As I gear up for these final exams, I figured it is time to wrap up 2023 with my last post of the year. Before I dive into the books, let me share some ML tales full of flops and wins that taught me a ton!

The Epic ML Mishap That Schooled Me

Imagine me, a uni newbie, thinking I would nail my first machine learning project. I jumped into Scikit learn, threw together a basic model, and it crashed harder than my sleep schedule during exam season. The culprit? Messy data. I skipped cleaning it, tossed it in, and hoped for the best nope!

That is when Pandas stepped in to save the day. I spent hours wrestling with missing values and odd outliers, learning the hard way that data prep is everything. Now I know why cleaning data is the unsung hero of ML. Newbies, take my advice do not skip this unless you love debugging tears!

Regression Victory My First Win

After that flop, I needed a boost. So I built a simple linear regression model to predict marks based on study hours using Scikit learn. With some clean data this time, it actually worked! Seeing the predictions match up felt like magic, like I turned math into a superpower.

It was not flawless my R squared could use some work but it showed me how to adjust features and read results without overthinking. A small win, but it pumped me up. If you are starting ML, try this it is the perfect beginner step.

Future Adventures in 2024

Exams are wrapping up, and 2023 is almost done, leaving me excited for what is next. I am dreaming of more ML experiments, neater code, and projects that do not fall apart. This semester taught me to push through bad data and tough slopes.

This might be my last post of the year, so thanks for sticking with me, fam! Share your exam tips or ML tricks in the comments I could use them. Here is a shair to sign off:

پڑھائی کا زور, کوڈ کا شور

سمسٹر ختم, اب نیا دور

— محمد طہ ناصر

Translation for my non Urdu friends: Study grind, code roar, semester ends, now a new era.

Happy holidays, see you in 2024!
