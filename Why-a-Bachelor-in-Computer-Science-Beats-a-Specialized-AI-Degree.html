The Software Engineering vs. Computer Science Misconception

  • Job Postings Mislead – Fresh grads often see job listings that discourage them...
  • Why core CS matters – CS helps you understand systems, debug problems, and grow.

The Pitfall of Narrow Specialization

  • Lock-in problem – AI-specific BS can limit future career switches.
  • Overlap – Most CS and AI programs overlap by 80%.

What Truly Matters in AI & ML Careers

  • System skills – Real-world ML is about deployment, not model-building.
  • DevOps + CS – Building real AI apps needs full-stack skill, not just theory.

Your Smart Approach

  1. Do a CS degree with AI electives/projects.
  2. Ensure your college covers core AI: Probabilities, RL, etc.

TL;DR

    CS > overly specialized BS in AI
  • You can specialize via electives/projects without locking yourself.
